# OpenReOC

**Free, open-access knowledge for Australian drone operators.**

Most RePL and ReOC holders rely on expensive consultants for things they could 
learn to do themselves — approvals, operational variations, procedures, CASA forms. 
The knowledge exists in public documents, but it's scattered, unclear, and hard to 
follow without experience.

This project documents my own learning and development process as I build procedures, 
operational approvals, and ReOC amendments for the organisations I work with. No 
confidential or company-specific information is disclosed — what you'll find here 
are the source documents I read, the regulatory references I relied on, the supporting 
documents I developed, and the thinking behind each decision.

Everything is updated as the work progresses, including feedback received
from CASA and industry practitioners, so the guides reflect what actually
works — not just theory. The goal is that any ReOC holder can follow the
same process and do it themselves.
