## Lessons Learned
*(updated as the process progresses)*

- Start with TMI 2024-01 as the primary reference, not the CASRs. 
  The TMI is where the pathway conditions, definitions, and practical 
  requirements live. CASR 101.245 and 101.280 tell you what you can't 
  do without approval; the TMI tells you how to get the approval.
- Identify your pathway and draft the ConOps early. Everything else 
  (energy calcs, procedures, consent forms) flows from knowing which 
  pathway you're pursuing and what operations you're applying for.
