## What CASA Looks For in an Application

Based on the TMI and industry practice, CASA assessors will evaluate:

### Operations Manual
- OONP procedures specific to Pathway 1 (including but not limited to standard and emergency procedures)
- Define what is and how to establish a controlled environment, including Access control procedures, observer roles and positioning
- Define the personnel responsible for delivering briefings and training.

### Consent Process
- Consent form template, including all TMI-required items
- Process for briefing active participants and obtaining consent documentation
- Age verification process
- Process for Withdrawal of consent

### Risk Assessment
- OONP-specific hazard identification and site assessment methodology
- Controlled environment failure modes (perimeter breach scenarios)

### Internal Training
- OONP-specific training syllabus
- Competency assessment
- Recurrency requirements

### Record Keeping
- OONP mission logs
- Retaining consent documentations
